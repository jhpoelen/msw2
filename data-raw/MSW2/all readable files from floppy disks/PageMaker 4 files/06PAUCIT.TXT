ORDER PAUCITUBERCULATA by Alfred L. Gardner

ORDER PAUCITUBERCULATA
SYNONYMS: Marsupialia. 

Family Caenolestidae Trouessart, 1898. Cat. Mamm. Viv.
     Foss., 2(5):1205.
COMMENTS: Reviewed by Marshall (1980) and Bublitz
     (1987). 

Caenolestes Thomas, 1895. Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist., ser. 6,
     16:367.
TYPE SPECIES: Hyracodon fuliginosus Tomes, 1863, by
     monotypy.
SYNONYMS: Hyracodon Tomes, 1863 (preoccupied). 

Caenolestes caniventer Anthony, 1921. Am. Mus. Novit.,
     20:6.
TYPE LOCALITY: Ecuador, El Oro, "El Chiral."
DISTRIBUTION: SW Ecuador and NW Per£.

Caenolestes convelatus Anthony, 1924. Am. Mus. Novit.,
     120:1.
TYPE LOCALITY: Ecuador, Pichincha, "Las Maquinas,
     Western Andes 7000 feet altitude, on trail from
     Aloag to Santo Domingo de los Colorados."
DISTRIBUTION: W Colombia and NW Ecuador.
SYNONYMS: barbarensis Bublitz, 1987. 

Caenolestes fuliginosus (Tomes, 1863). Proc. Zool. Soc.
     Lond., 1863:51.
TYPE LOCALITY: "Ecuador."
DISTRIBUTION: Colombia, Ecuador, and NW Venezuela.
SYNONYMS: centralis Bublitz, 1987; obscurus Thomas,
     1895; tatei Anthony, 1923. 

Lestoros Oehser, 1934. J. Mammal., 15:240.
TYPE SPECIES: Orolestes inca Thomas, 1917, by original
     designation.
SYNONYMS: Cryptolestes Tate, 1934 (preoccupied);
     Orolestes Thomas, 1917 (preoccupied). 

Lestoros inca (Thomas, 1917). Smithson. Misc. Coll.,
     68(4):3.
TYPE LOCALITY: Per£, Cuzco, "Torontoy."
DISTRIBUTION: S Andean Per£.
SYNONYMS: gracilis Bublitz, 1987. 

Rhyncholestes Osgood, 1924. Field Mus. Nat. Hist.
     Publ., Zool. Ser., 14:170.
TYPE SPECIES: Rhyncholestes raphanurus Osgood, 1924, by
     original designation.

Rhyncholestes raphanurus Osgood, 1924. Field Mus. Nat.
     Hist. Publ., Zool. Ser., 14:170.
TYPE LOCALITY: Chile, Biobio, "mouth of Rio Inio, south
     end of Chilo‚ Island."
DISTRIBUTION: SC Chile including Chilo‚ Isl.
SYNONYMS: continentalis Bublitz, 1987. 
COMMENTS: See Patterson and Gallardo (1987, Mammalian
     Species, 286).
