ORDER NOTORYCTEMORPHIA by Colin P. Groves

ORDER NOTORYCTEMORPHIA
SYNONYMS: Syndactyliformes.
COMMENTS: Recognized as an order by Aplin and Archer
     (1987) who proposed a new syncretic classification
     of the marsupials. 

Family Notoryctidae Ogilby, 1892. Cat. Aust. Mammalia,
     p. 5.
COMMENTS: Relationships unknown.

Notoryctes Stirling, 1891. Trans. R. Soc. S. Aust.,
     14:154.
TYPE SPECIES: Psammoryctes typhlops Stirling, 1889.
SYNONYMS: Neoryctes, Psammoryctes. 
COMMENTS: Psammoryctes is preoccupied (see Iredale and
     Troughton, 1934). 

Notoryctes caurinus Thomas, 1920. Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist.,
     ser. 9, 6:111.
TYPE LOCALITY: Western Australia, Wollal (= Wallal),
     Ninety Mile Beach.
DISTRIBUTION: NW Western Australia.
STATUS: Unknown.
COMMENTS: Separated from N. typhlops by Walton
     (1988:47).

Notoryctes typhlops (Stirling, 1889). Trans. R. Soc. S.
     Aust., 12:158.
TYPE LOCALITY: Australia, Northern Territory,
     Indracowrie, 100 mi. (161 km) from Charlotte
     Waters.
DISTRIBUTION: Western deserts from Ooldea (South
     Australia) to Charlotte Waters and NW Western
     Australia, Northern Territory.
STATUS: Thought to be rare, but no real data exist.
